About Us


TechScholar was formed in 2017 by Efosa Iguodala after discovering the gradual decline in practical ICT education in the primary and school level. It became fully functional in 2019. TechScholar is a division of I.G grant resources in 2017 which has investments in I.T, Project Management, Career Development, Branding and Event Management.
